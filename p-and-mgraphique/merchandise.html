About

On this page, you will be able to find and scroll through any and all merchandise available through the websites store. This merchandise is not the final product, but instead a blank canvas for your new and improved company logo. Any merchandise on this page is susceptible to change and the inventory may be rotated monthly to accommodate new ideas. All clothing sizes and measurements are measured in inches per, and are as accurate as can be. There is a 1 month free-return money-back warranty on all merchandise in case a problem arises.

Inventory

The merchandise that we stock on our online store consists of mugs, sweaters, hoodies, t-shirts, stickers, hats, etc. All inventory is susceptible to change and may not stay the same. If an item is sold-out, we will put a hold on that item until we can restock it. The shops stock refreshes every Tuesday at 5:00 PM MDT. Clothing is bought in wholesale through a retailer, any problems with clothing bought from this website can be resolved via email at pandmgraphics@Umail.com. All clothing is made of a high-quality and resilient 50/50 polyester-cotton mix, keep in mind that we are not responsible for any allergic reactions that this product might cause.

Hats

Our hats come in 10 different colors including black, white, tan, red, green, blue, gray, pink, purple, and off-white. They come in different styles as well, such as flat-brim, curved-brim, snapback, and flex fit; perfect for any occasion.

Shirts

Much like our hats, our premium T-shirts come in 10 different colors including black, white, tan, red, green, blue, gray, pink, purple, and off-white. Our shirts are a sweatproof, dry-wick 50/50 blend of cotton and polyester sure to leave you feeling cool all day.
