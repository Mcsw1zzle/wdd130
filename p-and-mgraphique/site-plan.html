Overview

Purpose

Our sole purpose and mission is to create an environment in which stress can be relieved, and creativity can be fostered. The goal is to guide and help you in the process of designing a logo for your company, as well as applying that logo to various degrees of merchandise and marketing.

Audience

This website is aimed at anyone looking to further their business. If you are a veteran entrepreneur, or just looking to take your first steps as an entrepreneur, this website was made for you and your creative business endeavors.

Home page

Welcome

Welcome to P&M Graphics. We are so glad you chose to start your business off on the right foot. If you are looking to be inspired creatively, get a new logo, and begin taking the first steps to globalizing your brand through merchandise, you have come to the right. Here, you can begin by using our database of logo templates/premade logos to find which company branding and style suits your business best. After selecting a logo you will finally be able to start applying it to high-quality, durable, and aesthetically pleasing merchandise. This merchandise includes, clothing, apparel, and everyday household items. Come back every month for new merchandise options.

About Us

The Inspiration

This inspiration for starting this website was one that I attribute to my education as a young adult. I am currently taking a class in web development and design for college, in order to further my education in the field of coding. Our final assignment for the semester would lead to the creation of this website and all of its content. Little did I know that an assignment spanning the course of a couple of months would be so impactful on me and my education, as well as my confidence as a coder and designer. I hope that one day I can go on to create many mighty and beautiful, fully functioning websites.

The Plan

This idea for this website came to me when I had a difficult time, about a year ago, coming up with my own logo for a project I had been working on regarding starting a small company. I am sure there are plenty of people out there just like me, who have a difficult time being creative, and could use some help from now and again. That is why I created a plan to design and code a website that could accommodate the less creative by giving them logo inspiration.

The Mission

As mentioned in the above paragraph, this website allows you to pick from a wide variety of logo templates/premade logos for your business. Once you have selected one that you like, you will be able to then pick out merchandise that you can apply that logo to. This opens up a great opportunity to take the first steps of marketing and advertising your business to a larger audience than you might currently be dealing with. This website aims to please, by taking some stress off of your plate, and getting you started on the right path to being a successful business owner.
